ShareFrame {{VERSION}} - Portable Windows build
================================================

Start ShareFrame
----------------
1. Keep this extracted ShareFrame folder on your D: drive.
2. Double-click ShareFrame.exe.
3. ShareFrame opens in your normal browser.
4. Keep the ShareFrame window open while recording. Close it to stop the app.

No account is required. Recordings stay on this machine and are private until
you create a guest link.

Storage
-------
ShareFrame stores recordings, thumbnails, backups, and private share state at:

  D:\ShareFrame\data

Temporary runtime files stay at:

  D:\ShareFrame\temp

The storage location can be changed with OPENCAST_DATA_ROOT, but ShareFrame
will only accept a D-drive path.
